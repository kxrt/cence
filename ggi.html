The CS for Graduating Girls Initiative @ CeNCE

  • For students who will be graduating from JCs at the end of 2023
  • Applicable students need only register for this initiative
  • There is no registration fee to join the initiative
  • Registered students may attend courses (in 2024) at discounted rates
  • Registered students may apply for internship opportunities (also in 2024)
    • Note that places will likely be competitive and only granted after taking offered courses
